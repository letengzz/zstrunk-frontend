# Three-axle 40,000L 4-compartment Carbon Steel Tanker Truck

![产品主图](/md/assets/image-20260129214046541.png)

## Overview

The **Three-axle 40,000L 4-compartment Carbon Steel Tanker Truck** is a high-performance bulk liquid transportation solution designed for safe and efficient delivery of petroleum products, chemical liquids, and other hazardous materials. Built with advanced manufacturing techniques and premium carbon steel, this tanker ensures exceptional durability and longevity.

## Key Features

### Robust Construction
- **Material**: High-quality carbon steel construction
- **Thickness**: 5mm-8mm tank body with reinforced structural design
- **Anti-corrosion Treatment**: Industrial-grade painting and coating system
- **Service Life**: 10-15 years with proper maintenance

### Advanced Engineering
- **Tank Capacity**: 40,000 liters maximum volume
- **Compartments**: 4 compartments for multi-product transportation
- **Discharge System**: Bottom loading/unloading with safety valves
- **Manhole**: Standard 500mm diameter with secure locking mechanism

### Safety Systems
- **Emergency Shut-off**: Pneumatic/Manual emergency valve system
- **Pressure Relief**: Venting system with flame arrestors
- **Spill Control**: Containment dikes and overflow protection
- **Braking System**: ABS/EBS with spring brake chambers
