# Water Tanker Truck 20000L

## Overview

The **Water Tanker Truck 20000L** is designed for efficient water transportation for construction sites, agricultural irrigation, and dust suppression. Built with durable carbon steel construction, this tanker ensures reliable performance in various working conditions.

## Key Features

### Robust Construction
- **Material**: High-quality carbon steel construction
- **Thickness**: 5mm-8mm tank body with reinforced structural design
- **Anti-corrosion Treatment**: Industrial-grade painting and coating system
- **Service Life**: 10-15 years with proper maintenance

### Advanced Engineering
- **Tank Capacity**: 20,000 liters maximum volume
- **Pump**: Self-priming pump included for easy discharge
- **Discharge System**: Quick discharge system for efficient operation
- **Manhole**: Standard 500mm diameter with secure locking mechanism

### Safety Systems
- **Emergency Shut-off**: Pneumatic/Manual emergency valve system
- **Pressure Relief**: Venting system
- **Braking System**: ABS/EBS with spring brake chambers
