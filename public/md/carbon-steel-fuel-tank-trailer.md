# Carbon Steel Fuel Tank Trailer

## Product Overview

The **Carbon Steel Fuel Tank Trailer** is built with high-strength Q345B carbon steel, designed for heavy-duty transportation of petroleum products. This robust trailer offers excellent durability and cost-effectiveness for demanding transportation operations.

## Key Features

- **Heavy-Duty Construction**: Q345B carbon steel for maximum durability
- **High Capacity**: Up to 45,000 liters capacity
- **Cost-Effective**: Competitive pricing with excellent value
- **Easy Maintenance**: Standard components widely available
- **Strong Load Bearing**: Suitable for rough road conditions
