# Mini Excavator XG815

## Overview

The **Mini Excavator XG815** is perfect for urban construction projects and tight spaces. Its compact design allows for easy transportation and maneuverability, making it ideal for various excavation tasks in confined areas.

## Key Features

### Compact Design
- **Operating Weight**: 15,000 kg
- **Bucket Capacity**: 0.6 m³
- **Zero Tail Swing**: Excellent maneuverability in tight spaces
- **Transport**: Easy to transport on standard trailers

### Powerful Performance
- **Engine Power**: 115 HP
- **Dig Depth**: Deep digging capability for its size
- **Hydraulic System**: Advanced hydraulic system for smooth operation
- **Fuel Efficiency**: Optimized fuel consumption

### Versatility
- **Adjustable Undercarriage**: Expands for stability, retracts for transport
- **Rubber Tracks**: Minimal ground damage
- **Foldable Boom**: Easy transport and storage
- **Quick Coupler**: Easy attachment changes
