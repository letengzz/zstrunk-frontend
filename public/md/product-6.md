# Bulk Cement Trailer 50 Ton

## Overview

The **Bulk Cement Trailer 50 Ton** is designed for efficient transportation of bulk cement and other powdered materials. Built with durable carbon steel construction, this trailer ensures reliable performance in various working conditions.

## Key Features

### Robust Construction
- **Material**: High-quality carbon steel construction
- **Thickness**: 5mm-8mm tank body with reinforced structural design
- **Anti-corrosion Treatment**: Industrial-grade painting and coating system
- **Service Life**: 10-15 years with proper maintenance

### Advanced Engineering
- **Tank Capacity**: 50,000 kg maximum payload
- **Discharge System**: Efficient discharge system for powdered materials
- **Dust Suppression System**: Prevents environmental contamination
- **Aerodynamic Design**: Reduces fuel consumption during transport
