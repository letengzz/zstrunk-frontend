# Asphalt Tank Trailer 40m³

## Overview

The **Asphalt Tank Trailer 40m³** is specialized for transporting hot asphalt at temperatures up to 200°C. Built with advanced thermal insulation and heating systems, this trailer ensures efficient asphalt delivery for road construction projects.

## Key Features

### Robust Construction
- **Material**: High-quality carbon steel construction
- **Thickness**: 6mm-10mm tank body with reinforced structural design
- **Insulation**: Rock wool insulation for temperature retention
- **Anti-corrosion Treatment**: Industrial-grade painting and coating system

### Advanced Engineering
- **Tank Capacity**: 40,000 liters (40m³)
- **Operating Temperature**: Up to 200°C
- **Heating System**: Efficient heating system for temperature maintenance
- **Discharge System**: Easy discharge system for asphalt

### Safety Systems
- **Safety Valves**: Pressure relief and safety valves
- **Thermal Insulation**: Rock wool insulation for temperature control
- **Easy Cleaning**: Designed for easy cleaning between loads
