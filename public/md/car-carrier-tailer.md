# Car Carrier Trailer

## Product Overview

The **Car Carrier Trailer** (also known as auto transport trailer) is designed for safe and efficient transportation of multiple vehicles. Featuring multi-deck design, it maximizes vehicle transport capacity.

## Key Features

- **Multi-Deck**: 2-3 levels available
- **Vehicle Capacity**: 6-20 vehicles
- **Safe Transport**: Secure lashing system
- **Hydraulic Loading**: Easy ramp operation
- **Durable**: Heavy-duty construction
