# Stainless Steel Tanker Trailer

## Product Overview

The **Stainless Steel Tanker Trailer** is designed for transporting food-grade liquids, pharmaceuticals, and corrosive chemicals. The high-quality stainless steel construction ensures product purity and corrosion resistance.

## Key Features

- **Food-Grade Quality**: SUS 304/316L stainless steel
- **Corrosion Resistance**: Excellent for aggressive chemicals
- **Easy Cleaning**: Mirror-finish interior surfaces
- **Hygienic Design**: Complies with food safety standards
- **Long Service Life**: Durable stainless construction

## Technical Specifications

| Item | Parameter |
|------|-----------|
| Overall Dimension | 11500×2500×3200mm |
| Tank Capacity | 35,000 - 40,000 Liters |
| Material | SUS 304 / SUS 316L |
| Thickness | 4-5mm |
| Axle | FUWA 13 tons × 3 |
| Surface Finish | Mirror/Polish |
