# 45000Liters Oil Tanker Trailer - Product Introduction

## Overview

The **45000Liters Oil Tanker Trailer** is a high-performance bulk liquid transportation solution designed for safe and efficient delivery of petroleum products, chemical liquids, and other hazardous materials. Built with advanced manufacturing techniques and premium materials, this trailer meets international safety standards and offers exceptional durability.

## Key Features

### Robust Construction
- **Material Options**: Carbon steel, stainless steel, or aluminum alloy可根据货物特性选择
- **Thickness**: 5mm-8mm tank body with reinforced structural design
- **Anti-corrosion Treatment**: Industrial-grade painting and coating system
- **Service Life**: 10-15 years with proper maintenance

### Advanced Engineering
- **Tank Capacity**: 45,000 liters maximum volume
- **Compartments**: 2-4 compartments for multi-product transportation
- **Discharge System**: Bottom loading/unloading with safety valves
- **Manhole**: Standard 500mm diameter with secure locking mechanism

### Safety Systems
- **Emergency Shut-off**: Pneumatic/Manual emergency valve system
- **Pressure Relief**: Venting system with flame arrestors
- **Spill Control**: Containment dikes and overflow protection
- **Braking System**: ABS/EBS with spring brake chambers
