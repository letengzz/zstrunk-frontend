# Shacman Dump Truck

## Product Overview

The **Shacman Dump Truck** is designed for heavy-duty hauling in mining, construction, and quarry operations. Featuring a robust chassis and reliable hydraulic system, it delivers exceptional performance in demanding conditions.

## Key Features

- **Heavy-Duty Chassis**: Shacman F3000/X3000
- **Powerful Engine**: WEICHAI 336-550 HP
- **Large Payload**: 25-60 tons
- **Fast Unloading**: Efficient hydraulic system
- **Durable Body**: High-strength steel
