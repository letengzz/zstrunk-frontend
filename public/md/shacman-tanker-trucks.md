# Shacman Tanker Trucks

## Product Overview

The **Shacman Tanker Truck** is built on the reliable Shacman chassis for transporting fuel, water, and chemical liquids. Featuring specialized tank designs, it ensures safe and efficient liquid transport.

## Key Features

- **Shacman Chassis**: F3000/X3000 platform
- **Various Capacities**: 5,000-25,000 liters
- **Specialized Tanks**: Fuel, water, chemical
- **Safety Features**: Comprehensive protection
- **Easy Operation**: Simple filling/discharge

## Technical Specifications

| Item | Parameter |
|------|-----------|
| Chassis | Shacman F3000/X3000 |
| Engine | WEICHAI 336-430 HP |
| Drive Form | 4×2 / 6×4 |
| Tank Capacity | 5,000 - 25,000 L |
