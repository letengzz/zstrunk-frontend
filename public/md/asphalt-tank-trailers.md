# Asphalt Tank Trailers

## Product Overview

The **Asphalt Tank Trailer** is designed for efficient transportation of hot asphalt, bitumen, and related road construction materials. Featuring thermal insulation and heating systems, it maintains product temperature during transport.

## Key Features

- **Temperature Maintenance**: Thermal insulation keeps asphalt hot
- **Heating Options**: Multiple heating system choices
- **Heavy-Duty Design**: Reinforced construction for hot products
- **Efficient Unloading**: Steam or thermal oil heating options
- **Safety Features**: Comprehensive safety systems for hot materials
