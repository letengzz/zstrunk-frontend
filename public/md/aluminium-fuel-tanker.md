# Aluminium Fuel Tanker

## Product Overview

The **Aluminium Fuel Tanker** is designed for efficient transportation of fuel, oil, and petroleum products. Featuring lightweight aluminum alloy construction, it offers excellent payload capacity and fuel efficiency for long-distance transportation needs.

## Key Features

- **Lightweight Design**: Aluminum construction reduces tare weight, increasing payload capacity
- **Corrosion Resistance**: Natural oxide layer provides excellent corrosion protection
- **Heat Dissipation**: Aluminum offers superior heat dissipation properties
- **Multiple Compartments**: Up to 8 compartments for carrying different fuel types
- **Easy Cleaning**: Smooth interior surfaces for easy cleaning and maintenance
