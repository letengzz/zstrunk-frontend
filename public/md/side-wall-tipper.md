# Side Wall Tipper

## Product Overview

The **Side Wall Tipper** allows unloading from the side, providing advantages in tight spaces or when loading/unloading from one side is required. The side dumping design offers large capacity and fast unloading.

## Key Features

- **Side Dumping**: Unload from either side
- **Large Capacity**: Voluminous design
- **Fast Unloading**: Short cycle time
- **Space Efficient**: No rear space needed
- **Stable Design**: Low center of gravity
