# Gas Tanker Trailer

## Product Overview

The **Gas Tanker Trailer** is designed for safe transportation of liquefied petroleum gas (LPG) and other compressed gases. Featuring pressure vessel construction and comprehensive safety systems, it meets international pressure vessel standards.

## Key Features

- **Pressure Vessel Design**: Meets ASME/ADR standards
- **Comprehensive Safety**: Multiple safety relief devices
- **Corrosion Protection**: Internal coating for gas purity
- **Precision Instruments**: Level and pressure gauges
- **Easy Operation**: Simple filling and discharge systems

## Technical Specifications

| Item | Parameter |
|------|-----------|
| Overall Dimension | 11000×2500×3200mm |
| Tank Capacity | 52,000 Liters (water capacity) |
| Working Pressure | 1.8 MPa |
| Test Pressure | 2.7 MPa |
| Material | Q345R Pressure Vessel Steel |
| Axle | FUWA 13 tons × 3 |
| Design Standard | ASME / ADR |
