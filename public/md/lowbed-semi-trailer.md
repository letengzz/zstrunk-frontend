# Lowbed Semi Trailer

## Product Overview

The **Lowbed Semi Trailer** (also known as low loader) is designed for transporting heavy machinery, construction equipment, and oversized cargo. Its low center of gravity ensures stable transportation of heavy loads.

## Key Features

- **Heavy Load Capacity**: Up to 100 tons
- **Low Center of Gravity**: Stable transport
- **Removable Gooseneck**: Easy loading
- **Various Configurations**: 2-4 axles
- **Durable Construction**: High-strength steel
