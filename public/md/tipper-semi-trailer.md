# Tipper Semi Trailer

## Product Overview

The **Tipper Semi Trailer** (dump trailer) features a hydraulic lifting system for self-unloading of bulk materials. Available in various configurations including side dump, rear dump, and multi-axle options.

## Key Features

- **Self-Unloading**: Hydraulic lift system
- **Fast Unloading**: Short cycle time
- **Various Configurations**: Side, rear, and bottom dump
- **Durable Body**: Hardox or equivalent steel
- **Easy Operation**: Simple control system
