# Wheel Excavator XG960

## Overview

The **Wheel Excavator XG960** offers excellent mobility and versatility for various construction applications. With 4-wheel drive and all-wheel steering, this excavator can easily navigate rough terrain and tight job sites.

## Key Features

### Mobility
- **4-Wheel Drive**: Excellent traction on rough terrain
- **All-Wheel Steering**: Enhanced maneuverability
- **Travel Speed**: High travel speed between job sites
- **Rubber Tires**: Minimal ground damage

### Versatility
- **360-Degree Swing**: Complete swing capability
- **Quick Coupler System**: Easy attachment changes
- **Multiple Attachments**: Bucket, breaker, grapple options
- **On-Road Capability**: Can travel on public roads
