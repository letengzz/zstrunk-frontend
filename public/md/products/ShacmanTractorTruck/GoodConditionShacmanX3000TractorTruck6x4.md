## Product Description

Shacman X3000 series 6X4 Container Tractor Truck has a high requirement for light weight body and a relatively small operating radius. Its main driver's seat adopts airbag seat, which pays more attention to the comfort of the occupants and meets people's demand for a comfortable cab. It has the hot spots of low dead weight and low fuel consumption, and has won the recognition of the majority of users with its high cost performance. This product can well meet the basic needs of the vehicle purpose and the driver and passengers.

## Product feature and application

Shacman X3000 6x4 Container Tractor Truck's cab top has undergone strict pressure resistance test, with excellent safety performance and better guarantee in product quality. Its thickened cab steel plate and side door crash beam design effectively enhance the passive safety of the cab. This tractor truck can meet basic transportation needs and tackle some complicated tasks.

## Product details

● The cab adopts European frame structure and has passed the most rigorous ECE-R29 crash test.

● Optimized shift operation: telescopic shaft shift operation can absorb vibration from the chassis, ensuring no spontaneous out of gear and control performance, improve the seal performance of the cab.

● Weichai Four-valve engine: strong power with large output torque, lower engine speed at maximum torque, which is the best power and explosive force in the industry.

● Fast transmission has dual intermediate shafts with high reliability; large speed ratio range, strong climbing ability, suitable for different working conditions; small speed ratio differential and high fuel economy.

● New generation electrical system: advanced CAN bus technology from Germany BOSCH with the function of automatic fault detection, the newly upgraded electrical system has been verified by the vehicle market of 12 million kilometers, with stable performance, safe and convenient operation.
