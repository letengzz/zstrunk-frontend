# Shacman Trucks

## Product Overview

**Shacman Trucks** (Shaanxi Automobile) are renowned heavy-duty trucks known for their reliability, durability, and excellent performance. We offer a complete range including dump trucks, tractor trucks, and tanker trucks built on Shacman chassis.

## Key Features

- **Quality Chassis**: Shacman original chassis
- **Powerful Engines**: WEICHAI or Cummins options
- **Reliable Transmission**: FAST or Fuller gearbox
- **Strong Axles**: Hande or Meritor axles
- **Global Service**: Extensive service network
