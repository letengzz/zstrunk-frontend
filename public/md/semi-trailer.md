# Semi Trailer

## Product Overview

Our **Semi Trailer** series offers comprehensive solutions for various transportation needs. From heavy-duty lowbed trailers for construction equipment to versatile tipper trailers for bulk materials, we provide reliable trailers for diverse applications.

## Key Features

- **Diverse Options**: Multiple types available
- **Heavy-Duty Design**: High load capacity
- **Quality Components**: FUWA axles, JOST landing gear
- **Customizable**: Various configurations
- **Global Standards**: CE/ISO certified

## Types Available

### Lowbed Semi Trailer
- For heavy machinery
- Low center of gravity
- Removable gooseneck optional

### Tipper Semi Trailer
- Self-unloading capability
- Hydraulic lifting system
- Various dump angles

### Fence Cargo Trailer
- Side wall design
- Ventilated cargo area
- Lightweight construction

### Side Wall Tipper
- Side dumping
- Large capacity
- Fast unloading

### Side Curtain Trailer
- Curtain side design
- Easy side loading
- Weather protection

### Car Carrier Trailer
- Multi-level design
- Safe vehicle transport
- Hydraulic loading

### Box Semi Trailer
- Enclosed cargo
- Weather protection
- Secure transport

### Full Drawbar Trailer
- Pull-type design
- High maneuverability
- Multiple axles

### Removable Gooseneck (RGN)
- Front loading
- Heavy equipment
- Hydraulic ramp
