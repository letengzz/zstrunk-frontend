# Chemical Tank Trailer

## Product Overview

The **Chemical Tank Trailer** is designed for safe transportation of hazardous and non-hazardous chemicals. Featuring specialized linings and comprehensive safety systems, it ensures safe handling of various chemical products.

## Key Features

- **Chemical Resistance**: Specialized linings available
- **Safety Compliance**: ADR/IMDG standards
- **Leak Prevention**: Multiple sealing systems
- **Corrosion Protection**: Internal coating options
- **Easy Decontamination**: Smooth interior surfaces

## Technical Specifications

| Item | Parameter |
