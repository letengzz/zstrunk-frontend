# Sinotruck HOWO

## Product Overview

**Sinotruck HOWO** (Huawai) is one of China's leading heavy-duty truck brands, known for its robust construction and reliable performance. Our HOWO trucks include tractor trucks, dump trucks, and tanker trucks built on the proven HOWO chassis.

## Key Features

- **Proven Chassis**: HOWO 7 series platform
- **Powerful Engines**: WEICHAI customized engines
- **Reliable Transmission**: FAST gearbox
- **Strong Axles**: STEYR or HANDE axles
- **Cost-Effective**: Competitive pricing
