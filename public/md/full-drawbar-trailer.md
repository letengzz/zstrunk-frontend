# Full Drawbar Trailer

## Product Overview

The **Full Drawbar Trailer** is a pull-type trailer connected via a drawbar to the tractor unit. This design offers high maneuverability and is ideal for applications requiring tight turning angles.

## Key Features

- **Pull-Type Design**: Drawbar connection
- **High Maneuverability**: Excellent turning
- **Multiple Axles**: Up to 4 axles
- **Heavy Load**: High payload capacity
- **Versatile**: Various body options
