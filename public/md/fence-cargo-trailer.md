# Fence Cargo Trailer

## Product Overview

The **Fence Cargo Trailer** features lightweight side walls designed for transporting palletized and general cargo. Its ventilated design makes it ideal for goods requiring air circulation.

## Key Features

- **Lightweight**: Aluminum or steel fence
- **Ventilated**: Air circulation design
- **Easy Loading**: Side loading capability
- **Versatile**: Various cargo types
- **Durable**: Robust frame construction
