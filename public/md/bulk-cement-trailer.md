# Bulk Cement Trailer

## Product Overview

The **Bulk Cement Trailer** is specially designed for efficient transportation of bulk cement, powdered materials, and other dry bulk commodities. Featuring an advanced air compressor discharge system, it ensures quick and complete unloading.

## Key Features

- **Efficient Unloading**: Air compressor system for fast discharge
- **Large Capacity**: 45 cubic meters volume
- **Dust-Free Operation**: Sealed loading and unloading
- **Durable Construction**: Wear-resistant internal coating
- **Low Maintenance**: Simple design with reliable components

## Standard Configuration

### Discharge System
- Air compressor system (BBA or equivalent)
- 110mm diameter discharge pipe
- Pneumatic discharge method
- Quick coupling fittings

### Tank Design
- Horizontal cylindrical tank
- Internal fluidization plates
- Pressure relief system
- Manhole for inspection

### Landing Gear
- 28 tons capacity
- Two-speed operation
- Heavy-duty construction

### Brake System
- Air brake system
- Spring parking brake
- ABS optional

## Optional Configurations

- Different capacity options (35-60 m³)
- Various compressor options
- Multiple discharge outlets
- Weighing systems
- Different axle configurations

## Applications

- Cement transportation
- Fly ash transport
- Powdered limestone
- Coal powder
- Other dry bulk materials
