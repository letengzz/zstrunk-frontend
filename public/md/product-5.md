# Chemical Tanker Truck 30000L

## Overview

The **Chemical Tanker Truck 30000L** is designed for safe transportation of various chemical liquids with advanced safety features and specialized construction materials.

## Key Features

### Robust Construction
- **Material**: High-quality stainless steel 316L
- **Lining**: PE/PP lining for chemical resistance
- **Thickness**: 5mm-8mm tank body with reinforced structural design
- **Double Wall Construction**: Enhanced safety for hazardous materials

### Advanced Engineering
- **Tank Capacity**: 30,000 liters maximum volume
- **Compartments**: Configurable compartments for multi-product transportation
- **Discharge System**: Bottom loading/unloading with safety valves
- **Manhole**: Standard 500mm diameter with secure locking mechanism

### Safety Systems
- **Pressure Relief Device**: Automatic pressure regulation
- **Leak Detection System**: Early warning for potential leaks
- **Emergency Shut-off**: Pneumatic/Manual emergency valve system
- **Chemical Resistant Lining**: Prevents contamination and corrosion
